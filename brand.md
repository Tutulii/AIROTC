## Voice

- Direct, confident, technical.
- Evidence-first: claims should point to logs, proofs, or transactions.
- Avoid hype language; prefer precise operational wording.

## Usage

Do:
- Use cobalt accent for primary actions and system emphasis.
- Keep dense telemetry surfaces readable with high contrast.
- Use mono for addresses, signatures, and numeric metrics.

Don't:
- Use playful or warm palettes for critical system states.
- Hide important states behind low-contrast muted text.
- Over-animate critical operational panels.
